Hingify help station

If you can't find a song, it is possible that the database is outdated in terms with Spotify.

There is 2 options that you can do to help:

  • 1. Adding it by listening to it on Spotify and pressing the button in the "How can I add a song with listening?" section.

  • 2. Adding it manually by copying an embed code in the "How can I find the Spotify Track ID?" section.

Make sure you have authorized Hingify to collect data from your Spotify Account.

If not, pressing the button may take you to a page where you have to authorize it first.

If you are on your Spotify launcher, it is just 4 simple steps:

  1. Right-click on your song
  2. Click "Share"
  3. Select "Embed Track"
  4. Click on "COPY" and just paste it here, we'll handle from there.
